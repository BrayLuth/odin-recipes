Lasagna

A italian pasta dish using long flat noodles layered with meats, cheese, and sauce.

Lasagna utilizes long flat noodles that are first boiled then layered in a baking dish with other key ingredients. these ingredients consist of ground beef and italian sausage, marinara sauce, and different types of cheeses. Layering looks like noodles then meat, sauce and cheese then noodles again and can be repeated for your desire thickness. then the whole dish is baked so the ingredients can cook some of which for the second time together.
